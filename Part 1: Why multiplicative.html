In the begining we are choosing for simplicity an addition model but later we will see that multiplicative model is a better choice.

We are also chosing the mean observation per month. There are two reasons behind it

  1. later on I want to show the seasonal decomposition. Now for years 2005 to 2019 and 365 days in each year, we will have thousands of days and hence the entire seasonal pattern will be dense and hard to visualize.
  2. Lastly, we would like to seasonal adjusted value and it will be hard to observe if we have seasonal adjusted value where the season is each day as we will have 365 seasons in a single frame.
